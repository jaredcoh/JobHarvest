Welcome to JobHarvest, the extension that helps your job search!
This uses the job alerts from popular job boards to find you jobs

Steps to make it work:
1. Log into LinkedIn, Glassdoor, or Indeed
2. Setup your respective job alerts
3. Whenever a job alert email comes in, click the extension and the "search your email" button to organize the information into a formatted table

You can organize the columns and sort/delete the rows.
You can then copy the table to bring it into your own spreadsheet
You can also add your sheets URL and tab name to send it to the spreadsheet automatically!

Soon, you will be able to have the program check your email and send it to your preferred spreadsheet automatically!

Happy Searching!
